Zurich Technology Delivery Center

Zurich Technology Delivery Center (also known as TDC) born in Barcelona in 2006 as part of the Zurich Insurance Group.

Zurich Technology Delivery Center provides the latest technologies & solutions to local and global business units with the Zurich Insurance group.

Our main objective is to offer the most innovative and disruptive business solutions always with the highest quality standards.

Technology Events

Internal

Zurich Digital League

Zurich Digital League is an event where teams around the world compete and present their digital solutions. It is about taking the best solutions across the group and adopting them at scale in other businesses.

Internal

Digital Experience Week

DEW is aimed to make the most out of our digital experiences. A week full of events to help employees discover what tools, knowledge and initiatives enable our global digital experiences.

External

Zurich Innovation Championship

Zurich Innovation Championship is a worldwide challenge for entrepreneurs, where the most innovative start-ups benefit from entering our co-creation implementation lab, where they work alongside Zurich, to provide each team the best opportunity to succeed.
